What problems we are solving ?

NovosEdge aims to solve several challenges in modern agriculture through its innovative technology and platform:


Resource Optimization

NovosEdge allows farmers to optimize their use of resources such as water, fertilizers, and pesticides, reducing waste and improving overall efficiency. This addresses the issue of resource scarcity and ensures sustainable farming practices.

Yield and Quality Improvement

NovosEdge tailors stage-wise crop-specific farming practices, resulting in higher yields and improved produce quality. By providing farmers with data-driven insights and precise approaches, NovosEdge helps maximize productivity and profitability.

Cost Reduction

NovosEdge ensures precise and efficient use of inputs like fertilizers, pesticides, and water, leading to reduced input costs and increased profitability for farmers. This addresses the financial burden on farmers and makes agriculture more economically viable.

With its data-driven approach, NovosEdge analyzes weather forecasts and pest life cycles, providing farmers with timely alerts and predictions for outbreaks. By offering preventive measures, NovosEdge helps farmers avoid losses and minimize risks.

NovosEdge plays a pivotal role in promoting sustainable agricultural practices by minimizing the use of chemicals, reducing soil erosion, water pollution, and greenhouse gas emissions. This addresses the environmental impact of conventional farming methods and promotes eco-friendly approaches.

Enhanced Decision-Making

NovosEdge provides farmers with real-time data and analytics, empowering them to make informed decisions about their operations. This helps address uncertainties and enables farmers to implement effective strategies for improved outcomes.

Our Products

AgriDoot

AgriDoot is an innovative IoT device designed for seamless farmland integration. Using multiple sensors, it collects real-time data on crop-specific atmospheric and soil conditions. This data is sent to a dedicated cloud server, where proprietary algorithms analyse it, considering the crop#39;s growth stage and microclimatic forecast. The system predicts irrigation and fertilizer requirements, along with potential pest issues, delivering customized, actionable suggestions directly to the farmer#39;s mobile phone throughout the entire crop production cycle.

Foliage Detect

The mobile application for plant disease detection allows users to click pictures of affected plant foliage for analysis. Utilizing advanced image recognition and machine learning, the app identifies various plant diseases in real-time. With an extensive database, it provides instant feedback on the detected disease and suggests relevant solutions for effective management. Its user-friendly interface ensures easy navigation, making it accessible to farmers and gardeners. Additionally, the app connects users with Agronomist for personalized advice. Overall, this innovative tool empowers users to protect their crops and plants, promoting agricultural productivity and healthier vegetation.
